March 6, 2025 • 5:00 PM

MIT Student Center Steps

84 Massachusetts Avenue, Cambridge

On March 6, 2025 at 5pm, The MIT Rally for Gender-Inclusive Rights will inaugurate a new platform for visibility, solidarity, and advocacy of the feminist and trans communities. Join us on the steps of MIT's Student Center at 84 Massachusetts Avenue, in a safe, protected space.

You will hear from experienced activists, describing what they've learned from long-standing efforts to fight the forces so dangerously in power right now. Community members whose rights are endangered by gender-based discrimination and persecution will have the chance to speak up, share their unique experiences, and help raise calls to action in the name of their struggles.

You need bring nothing but yourself, as water and basic aid will be provided, but feel free to bring any signs or other non-violent messages that express why you choose to come and fight today, and going forwards.
